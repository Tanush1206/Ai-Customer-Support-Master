# Billing & Refunds FAQ

Everything you need to know about managing your Nimbus subscription — changing plans, updating payment, viewing invoices, cancelling, and getting a refund. You can handle all of this yourself from **Settings → Billing**.

## Plans at a glance

Nimbus offers the following paid plans (USD):

- **Plus** — $11.99/mo or $119.88/yr ($9.99/mo billed annually). 2 TB storage.
- **Family** — $19.99/mo or $199.88/yr. Up to 6 members with 2 TB each, plus parental controls.
- **Business** — $18/user/mo (annual) or $20/user/mo (monthly), minimum 3 users. 5 TB pooled per user, admin console, SSO/SAML, audit logs, and priority support.
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing, with options like unlimited storage, SCIM provisioning, eDiscovery/legal hold, and a 99.9% uptime SLA.

There's also a **Free** plan ($0, 5 GB) you can return to at any time.

## Changing your plan

### How do I upgrade or downgrade?

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Select the plan you want to switch to.
3. Confirm the change.

**Upgrades** take effect immediately so you get the higher tier's storage and features right away. **Downgrades** apply at your next renewal date, so you keep your current plan's benefits until the period you've already paid for ends.

### How does proration work?

When you upgrade, the change is **prorated immediately**. You're charged the difference between what you've already paid and the cost of the new plan for the remainder of your current billing period — so you only pay for what you use.

Downgrades aren't prorated. Since they apply at the next renewal, there's no mid-cycle adjustment.

### Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?

Yes. Annual billing **saves about 17% — roughly two months free** compared to paying monthly. For example, Plus is $11.99/mo monthly, or the equivalent of $9.99/mo when billed annually ($119.88/yr).

To switch, choose the monthly or annual option for your plan under **Settings → Billing**.

## Payment & invoices

### How do I update my payment method?

1. Open **Settings → Billing**.
2. Find your saved card and select the option to update it.
3. Enter your new card details and save.

Your next charge will use the updated card.

### Where can I find my invoices?

All of your past invoices are available under **Settings → Billing**. You can view and download them there at any time for your records or expense reporting.

## Cancelling

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Choose to cancel your plan.
3. Confirm the cancellation.

You can cancel anytime. After cancelling, **your access continues until the end of the period you've already paid for** — you won't lose your paid features immediately. When the period ends, your account moves to the Free plan.

Keep in mind the Free plan includes 5 GB of storage. If you're storing more than that, you'll want to free up space or move files before your paid period ends.

## Refunds

### What is the 14-day refund policy?

Nimbus offers a **14-day money-back guarantee on your first purchase of a paid plan**. If you're not satisfied within 14 days of that first purchase, you can request a refund.

This applies to the initial purchase of a paid plan. To request a refund, reach out through your support channel (see **Getting more help** below).

## Failed payments

### What happens if my payment fails?

If a scheduled payment doesn't go through, Nimbus will **retry the charge for 7 days**. This gives you time to fix the problem — usually by updating your card.

To resolve a failed payment:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Update your payment method with a valid card.

If payment still hasn't succeeded after the **7-day retry window**, your account is **downgraded to the Free plan** (5 GB storage). Your files aren't deleted, but features and storage above the Free limits become unavailable until you resubscribe.

## A note on storage when changing plans

Storage limits differ significantly between plans:

- **Free** — 5 GB
- **Plus / Family** — 2 TB (Family is 2 TB per member, up to 6 members)
- **Business** — 5 TB pooled per user

Storage is shared across all your files, backups, and Nimbus Paper docs. Before downgrading or letting a plan lapse, make sure your usage fits within the lower plan's limit. Deleted files remain recoverable from **Trash** within your retention window (30 days on Free/Plus/Family, 365 days on Business).

## Getting more help

If you have a billing question this FAQ doesn't answer:

- **Free, Plus, and Family** — email and chat support (typical response within 24–48 hours).
- **Business** — priority support.
- **Enterprise** — phone support and a dedicated Customer Success Manager.

You can also check the Nimbus status page at **status.nimbus.example** for any service updates.
