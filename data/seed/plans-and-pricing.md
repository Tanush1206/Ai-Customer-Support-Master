# Nimbus Plans & Pricing

Nimbus is a cloud storage and collaboration platform for files, sync, sharing, Nimbus Paper docs, and Vault. It works on the web, on the desktop app (Windows, macOS, Linux), and on mobile (iOS and Android). This guide walks through every Nimbus plan, what it includes, how billing works, and how to choose the right plan for you or your team.

## Plans at a Glance

Nimbus offers five plans: **Free**, **Plus**, **Family**, **Business**, and **Enterprise**. Free is for individuals getting started, Plus is for power users who need more space, Family covers a household, Business is built for teams that need administration and security controls, and Enterprise adds scale, compliance, and dedicated support.

| Plan | Price | Storage | Max file size | Version history | Users |
|------|-------|---------|---------------|-----------------|-------|
| Free | $0 | 5 GB | 2 GB | 30 days | 1 |
| Plus | $11.99/mo or $119.88/yr ($9.99/mo billed annually) | 2 TB | 50 GB | 180 days | 1 |
| Family | $19.99/mo or $199.88/yr | 2 TB per member | 50 GB | 180 days | Up to 6 |
| Business | $20/user/mo monthly, or $18/user/mo annual | 5 TB pooled per user | 100 GB | 365 days | Min. 3 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Optional unlimited | — | — | — |

Storage is shared across all your files, backups, and Nimbus Paper docs.

## Plan Details

### Free

The Free plan costs **$0** and is a great way to try Nimbus.

- 5 GB of storage
- Maximum file size of 2 GB
- 30-day version history and trash
- 1 user
- Basic sharing links (no password or expiry)
- Shared-link traffic up to 20 GB/day

**Best for:** Individuals who want to test Nimbus or keep a small set of files in sync.

### Plus

Plus is **$11.99/month**, or **$119.88/year** — which works out to **$9.99/month when billed annually**.

- 2 TB of storage
- Maximum file size of 50 GB
- 180-day version history
- Password-protected and expiring share links
- Nimbus Transfer up to 100 GB
- Smart Sync to keep files online-only until you open them
- Document scanner on mobile
- Shared-link traffic up to 1 TB/day

**Best for:** Power users and professionals who need substantial storage and stronger sharing controls.

### Family

Family is **$19.99/month**, or **$199.88/year**.

- Up to 6 members, each with 2 TB of their own storage
- Family parental controls
- All Plus features for every member
- Shared-link traffic up to 1 TB/day

**Best for:** Households that want individual storage for each person plus parental controls, all on one plan.

### Business

Business is **$20/user/month** billed monthly, or **$18/user/month** billed annually, with a minimum of 3 users.

- 5 TB pooled per user
- Maximum file size of 100 GB
- 365-day version history and trash
- Admin console for managing the team
- SSO/SAML sign-in and audit logs
- Team folders
- Priority support
- Shared-link traffic up to 4 TB/day

**Best for:** Teams that need centralized administration, single sign-on, audit logging, and faster support.

### Enterprise

Enterprise uses **custom pricing** tailored to your organization.

- Optional unlimited storage
- SSO plus SCIM provisioning
- eDiscovery and legal hold
- A dedicated Customer Success Manager
- Phone support
- 99.9% uptime SLA

**Best for:** Large organizations with compliance, provisioning, and support requirements at scale.

## Billing

You can pay monthly or annually. **Annual billing saves about 17% — roughly two months free** compared with paying monthly.

A few things to know about how changes take effect:

- **Upgrades** are prorated and applied immediately.
- **Downgrades** take effect at your next renewal.
- Every first purchase of a paid plan includes a **14-day money-back refund**.

To manage your subscription:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Update your card, view invoices, or change your plan.
3. Cancel anytime — your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

If a payment fails, Nimbus retries for **7 days** before downgrading the account to Free.

## Storage and Retention

Storage limits depend on your plan: 5 GB on Free, 2 TB on Plus and Family, and 5 TB pooled per user on Business.

Deleted files are recoverable from Trash within your plan's retention window — **30 days** on Free, Plus, and Family, and **365 days** on Business. Remember that storage is shared across all your files, backups, and Nimbus Paper docs, and that mobile uploads also count toward your quota.

Shared-link daily traffic limits scale with your plan as well: 20 GB/day on Free, 1 TB/day on Plus and Family, and 4 TB/day on Business.

## Support by Plan

- **Free, Plus, and Family:** Email and chat support, with a 24–48 hour response time.
- **Business:** Priority support.
- **Enterprise:** Phone support and a dedicated Customer Success Manager.

You can always check current system status at **status.nimbus.example**.

## Which Plan Is Right for You?

- Choose **Free** if you're new to Nimbus or only need a few gigabytes.
- Choose **Plus** if you want 2 TB, larger files, Smart Sync, and password-protected or expiring links.
- Choose **Family** if up to six people in your household each need their own 2 TB plus parental controls.
- Choose **Business** if your team needs admin controls, SSO/SAML, audit logs, and priority support (minimum 3 users).
- Choose **Enterprise** if you need optional unlimited storage, SCIM provisioning, legal hold and eDiscovery, a dedicated CSM, and a 99.9% uptime SLA.
